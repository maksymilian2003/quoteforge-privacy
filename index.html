Your data never leaves your device

QuoteForge stores all data locally on your iPhone using Apple's CoreData framework. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. No account required. No servers. No analytics.

📱 Information you enter in the app

QuoteForge allows you to enter and store the following information locally on your device:

  • Your business name, contact details, logo, and VAT number
  • Client names, company names, email addresses, and phone numbers
  • Estimate and invoice data including line items, prices, and totals
  • Saved service items and templates

This information is stored exclusively using Apple's CoreData framework on your device. It is not uploaded to any server, cloud service, or third party.

📧 Email sending

When you choose to send an estimate by email, QuoteForge uses your device's built-in Mail app (via Apple's MessageUI framework). The email is composed and sent entirely through your own email account.

We do not have access to the content of emails you send, your email account credentials, or your contact list. The PDF attachment is generated on-device and passed directly to your Mail app.

🖼️ Photo library access

QuoteForge may request access to your photo library solely to allow you to select a business logo. This access is optional — the app functions fully without it.

  • We only access photos you explicitly select
  • Selected images are stored locally on your device only
  • We never scan, index, or upload your photo library

🔗 Third-party services

QuoteForge does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or data collection services. There are no SDKs from Facebook, Google, Firebase, Mixpanel, or similar services embedded in this app.

The app does not display advertisements.

👶 Children's privacy

QuoteForge is a business tool intended for use by adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

⚖️ Your rights (GDPR & UK GDPR)

As all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control at all times:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app
  • Delete: Delete individual records or uninstall the app to remove all data
  • Portability: Export estimates as PDF at any time
  • Correction: Edit any data directly within the app

Since we do not collect or store any data on our servers, we are unable to retrieve, provide, or delete data that exists only on your device.

🗓️ Data retention

Your data is retained on your device for as long as the app is installed. When you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed by iOS. We do not retain any copies.

If you use iCloud Backup, your app data may be included in your device backup according to Apple's privacy policy. This is controlled by your iOS settings, not by QuoteForge.

📝 Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

For significant changes, we will include a notice in the app update release notes on the App Store.
